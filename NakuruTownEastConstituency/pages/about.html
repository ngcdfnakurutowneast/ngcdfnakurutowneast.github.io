About NG-CDF NTEC

NTEC is a national government organisation which deals with Constituency Development Funds
for Nakuru town east Constituency.

Our Vision

A prosperous constituency with equitable socio-economic development across all wards.


Our Mission

To deliver quality services to our clients through optimal use of Available resources to improve
the quality of life of our constituency.
